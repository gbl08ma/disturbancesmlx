Privacy policy

Last update: November 14th, 2017 | Versão em português

The UnderLX Team (in Portuguese, "Equipa do UnderLX") ("Team") is a group of people who operate voluntarily, individually and without commercial interests, in the area of Information Technology. The activities of this group have a special focus in the area of Urban Public Transport, thus applying information technology techniques and knowledge to existing Urban Public Transport networks ("PTNs").

The goal of the Team is to improve, through their activity, the user experience of these networks. In addition, this group has no official connection, support or endorsement of any company or entity, including PTN operators.

The Team is responsible by the website perturbacoes.pt ("Website"), as well as by the UnderLX application ("App"), and undertakes to ensure the privacy of any personal data collected and/or transmitted online. The security and privacy of the Website and App user data are central concerns in the Team's activity.

Because we respect the rules regarding privacy and protection of personal and personally identifiable data, we prepared this document, in order to let you know how the information you make available will be taken care of, by the Team and their information systems.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, as using the Website or the App requires knowing and accepting the terms here described. The Team reserves the right to change their Privacy Policy, therefore we recommend reading this document regularly.

This document is organized in such a way as to answer the following questions:

  1. What user data is collected?
  2. When and how do we collect user data?
  3. Who is responsible by collecting and processing data?
  4. For how long do we keep the data?
  5. Does the Website use cookies?
  6. How is the collected information used?
  7. What security procedures are associated with the App and the Website, that ensure user data protection against undue access and modification?
  8. In what way can the users obtain, correct or delete the information they previously made available through the App?
  9. Miscellaneous points you should know regarding our Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy describes faithfully the privacy policy obeyed by the App and by the Website. If you have any question or doubt regarding any of the statements presented here, you can reach us via email through the address underlx@tny.im.

1. What user data is collected?

Both the App and the Website avoid collecting data about users without their explicit consent. In this sense, the Website does not collect information about the browser, browsing history or internet connection of the users, except when that is necessary as a means for solving technical problems or limiting access to Abusive users, as defined in the Terms.

None of the information submitted through the App is associated with any personal user data, as this type of data is not collected. The data collected and submitted by the application is associated with a pseudorandom ID. This ID is automatically assigned to each user when the App is installed. Should the user wish to submit data under a different ID, he can do so by reinstalling the App.

The data submitted when using the App depends on the settings the user has selected. Os dados submetidos ao utilizar a Aplicação dependem das definições que o Utilizador tiver escolhido para a mesma. Thus, we will describe all kinds of data users may eventually submit: trip logs, reports of problems in PTNs, and error reports or suggestions about the service provided by the App and the Website.

In terms of trip logs, the following data is collected: stations or stops visited in each trip, the order in which they were visited (that is, the path the user has taken) and the time of entry and exit at each station or stop.

In termos of reports of problems in PTNs, the data submitted matches perfectly the data provided by the user in the act of reporting, which may be eventually supplemented by the current user location at the station or stop level.

2. When and how do we collect user data?

As explained in the previous answer, the Website does not collect data about the users, except when that is necessary for troubleshooting technical issues or limiting access to Abusive Users.

In these exceptional circumstances, data collection will always be transparent to the user, only occurring in our servers, and only during Website usage. When troubleshooting, data will be discarded at the end of the troubleshooting session. When dealing with Abusive Users, data may be saved in a secure way and never made public, for future reference.

The App only collects data through its interactive use by the user. If, in the App, the background location setting is enabled, the App will also collect data in an automatic fashion, without user intervention, and only when he is using PTNs indicated by the App as supported, and only if the remaining settings and specifications of the device where the App is being executed allow.

The data collected by the App will be submitted along the trip and after its end, or saved for deferred submission, if an internet connection is not available.

3. Who is responsible by collecting and processing data?

The Team is the sole responsible for collecting and processing user data, as submitted by the App.

The individual trip logs and other data related to user location will never be made public, shared or sold to other entities.

4. For how long do we keep the data?

Depending on the type of data and its purpose, data is kept for different time periods, from a few seconds to multiple years. Generally, the higher the granularity of the data, the shorter the period for which they will be kept. At this moment, trip logs are kept for an infinite amount of time; this situation may change once the requirements and technical procedures allow.

5. Does the Website use cookies?

The Website does not use cookies to provide its core features. However, to be able to provide a more customized service, the Website includes external elements, namely, social network sharing elements. These external elements may use cookies.

Cookies are small pieces of information sent by Web servers, that are stored in the browser of the visitors, and are then sent by the browser at a later time, when accessing the websites that sent them.

The Team is not responsible by the cookies produced by external elements, whose sole responsibility is of the associated services.

6. How is the collected information used?

The collected information serves the following purposes:

  • Improving the routing, waiting time and route cost algorithms;
  • Allowing more efficient location of the vehicles of the supported PTNs and the more precise calculation of vehicle waiting times;
  • Informing App and Website users of possible problems or changes to the services provided by the operators of the supported PTNs;
  • Contributing to a global usage record of the supported PTNs, independently of the records eventually published by the operators of these networks;
  • Improving the App and its features.

The anonymous data provided by the users will be used exclusively for purposed connected to the service provided by the App and the Website, as well as the production of informative reports, to be published on the Website, through mass data analysis. None of the information provided will be sold, rented or shared with third parties. We will not publish data that can possibly allow for identifying individual users based on their individual public transport usage patterns.

7. What security procedures are associated with the App and the Website, that ensure user data protection against undue access and modification?

A Equipa toma todas as precauções necessárias para que os dados recolhidos através da utilização da Aplicação e do Website, e informações extraídas dos mesmos, sejam mantidos em segurança durante a sua transmissão e armazenamento.

No que toca à utilização do Website, os Utilizadores têm a opção de lhe aceder através de uma ligação que utilize o protocolo HTTP sobre o protocolo SSL (HTTPS), permitindo este último protocolo que os dados sejam transmitidos cifrados, de forma a garantir a sua confidencialidade e integridade, e que a autenticidade do servidor possa ser verificada. Anula-se, assim, o risco de interceção ou manipulação de dados durante o transporte.

A Equipa compromete-se a manter a configuração do protocolo SSL nos seus servidores atualizada de acordo com as melhores práticas internacionais.

Em relação à comunicação entre a Aplicação e os servidores da Equipa, a mesma realiza-se exclusivamente através do protocolo HTTPS descrito acima, verificando-se as mesmas propriedades e garantias.

Sobre a proteção dos dados contra acesso e alteração indevida, tanto o Website como o servidor de suporte à Aplicação foram desenhados de forma a impedir a libertação de dados mais sensíveis, nomeadamente registos de viagem.

Para submeter, aceder ou alterar registos de viagem, comunicações de problemas, ou outro tipo de dados que fiquem associados a uma instalação da Aplicação, esta autentica-se perante o servidor, tendo acceso apenas aos dados que dizem respeito a essa instância da Aplicação. Esta autenticação utiliza credenciais obtidas de forma segura aquando da instalação da Aplicação, através de ligação HTTPS.

A Equipa não se responsabiliza por eventuais danos decorrentes de proteção insuficiente dos aparelhos onde a Aplicação esteja instalada, e que permitam a obtenção ou manipulação de informação sensível, ou das credenciais mencionadas.

Se tiver qualquer dúvida relativa à segurança dos dados ou dos sistemas como um todo, ou para reportar vulnerabilidades de segurança, poderá contactar a nossa equipa de segurança por email através do endereço security@tny.im.

8. In what way can the users obtain, correct or delete the information they previously made available through the App?

Tendo em conta as condições de utilização da Aplicação e do Website, o Utilizador compromete-se a fornecer os seus registos de utilização para melhorar os serviços prestados pela Equipa. Os registos de viagem poderão ser corrigidos no final de cada viagem (se essa definição for ativada pelo Utilizador), ou até 7 dias após a sua conclusão, período após o qual a sua correção ficará bloqueada.

Neste momento, a opção para apagar registos de viagem apenas apaga o registo na Aplicação, e não no servidor da Aplicação, pelo que se o utilizador pretender apagar dos registos da Equipa parte ou a totalidade das informações submetidas por ele, deverá contactar-nos através do endereço underlx@tny.im.

9. Miscellaneous points you should know regarding our Privacy Policy.

Hiperligações (Links)

A Aplicação e o Website contêm hiperligações (links) para outros sites externos, que estão regidos por outras políticas de privacidade. A Equipa não é responsável pelas políticas de privacidade desses mesmos sites nem pela sua atividade. Recomendamos que os utilizadores da Aplicação e do Website consultem as políticas de privacidade próprias dos sites externos. A Política de Privacidade aqui descrita apenas se aplica à Aplicação e ao Website.

Alteração de Condições de Utilização da Aplicação e Website

Sempre que as Condições de Utilização ou Política de Privacidade da Aplicação ou do Website forem alteradas, a Equipa irá informar os seus Utilizadores através de mensagens na própria Aplicação e Website.

Autorização para Utilizações Diversas

Se a Equipa pretender utilizar os dados dos Utilizadores para fins distintos daqueles inicialmente propostos neste documento, solicitará uma nova autorização aos Utilizadores, através dos meios que julgar necessários.

Segurança de Dados

Os dados anónimos recolhidos estão integrados numa base de dados e apenas os resultados da sua análise estatística serão publicados, de tal maneira que seja impossível associar um determinado conjunto de dados a um Utilizador singular.
